order

Whether you are young or young at heart, in Christian ministry, or simply interested in introducing a friend to Jesus...

You can easily share God’s message of hope through the Word One to One.

the Word One to One packs

The Word One to One is available in Australia from koorong.com and matthiasmedia.com.au

You can download the order form.

Each pack contains 2 copies of each book, one for you and one for your friend. So if your church needs 50 copies, buy 25 packs.

By buying The Word One to One you will also be helping to fund further editions on different books of the Bible.
